About Us

Independent Investment Excellence

Founded in 2019, Pine Insights is a fully independent investment firm, operating exclusively with proprietary capital. Our focus spans technology, consumer markets, finance and pharmaceuticals, where we apply rigorous qualitative research and sophisticated quantitative analysis to make informed investment decisions across global and Indian markets.

Our Founder

Vikram Negi

Vikram Negi founded Pine Insights in 2019, bringing over two decades of leadership in financial services, e-commerce, and technology. He previously served as Chief Digital Officer at Angel One during its pre-IPO phase and Head of Product for eBay India, where he led the platform through its $1.1B acquisition by Walmart.

His career includes more than a decade of foundational leadership at Amazon, AOL and Oracle, alongside entrepreneurial roles at startups like BabyOye and Digite. Vikram holds a B.Tech in Computer Science from IIT Kanpur, an MBA from IIM Calcutta, and did postgraduate research at Northwestern University.
